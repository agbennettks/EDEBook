27. The Laplace transform of the solution is $$ \frac{1-\exp(-s)}{s(1+\exp(-s))(s^2+\pi^2)} $$ At $s=\pm i\pi$ both the $s^2+\pi^2$ term and the $1+\exp(-s)$ term are zero, so there is a double pole. Resonance is reasonable because the equation is undamped with natural (circular) frequency $\pi,$ hence the natural period is 2, and the square wave has period 2.
